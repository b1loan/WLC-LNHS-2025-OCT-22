Honor of Completion

Field Study 1 & 2 — Western Leyte College of Ormoc City, Inc.

About

This Event is about the Field Study Students of Western Leyte College of Ormoc City, Inc. for successfully completing all the required tasks and assignments given as well as the 240 hours minimum requirement to finish Field Study 1 & 2 Courses as per Joint CHED-DepEd Memorandum Order No. 01, s. 2021 and implemented by DepEd Ormoc City Division Memorandum No. 160, s. 2022.

Having deployed from July 21, 2025 to October 31, 2025. Will be held this 22nd day of October, Two Thousand Twenty-five at Lilo-an National High School, Barangay Lilo-an, Ormoc City, Leyte, Philippines.
