Certificate of Completion

Field Study 1 & 2 Recognition Ceremony

This event honors the Field Study Students of Western Leyte College of Ormoc City, Inc. for successfully completing all the required tasks and assignments, as well as the 240-hour minimum requirement to finish Field Study 1 & 2 Courses in accordance with Joint CHED-DepEd Memorandum Order No. 01, s. 2021, and DepEd Ormoc City Division Memorandum No. 160, s. 2022.

Deployment Period: July 21, 2025 – October 31, 2025
Date of Event: October 22, 2025
Venue: Lilo-an National High School, Barangay Lilo-an, Ormoc City, Leyte, Philippines.

“In celebration of dedication, perseverance, and excellence — Congratulations, Field Study Students of 2025!”
